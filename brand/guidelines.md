# Lemma Brand Guidelines

## Core Identity
Lemma is an open-source, AI-native GRC platform that treats compliance as a rigorous, provable engineering discipline.

Our brand visual identity reflects **mathematical precision, structural engineering, and hacker ethos**. We strictly avoid "friendly corporate SaaS" tropes, rounded corners, and pastel colors. 

## The Palette

| Name | Hex | RGB | HSL | Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Void Black** | `#0A0A0A` | `10, 10, 10` | `0, 0%, 4%` | Base background, deep space. |
| **Chalk White** | `#FFFFFF` | `255, 255, 255` | `0, 0%, 100%` | Primary text, heavy structural lines. |
| **Terminal Green** | `#00FF41` | `0, 255, 65` | `135, 100%, 50%` | Primary accent, the "therefore" dots, active states, matrix green. |
| **Theorem Yellow** | `#EAB308` | `234, 179, 8` | `45, 93%, 47%` | Warnings, pending states, highlights. |
| **Structure Gray** | `#333333` | `51, 51, 51` | `0, 0%, 20%` | Inactive structural elements, bounding boxes. |

## Typography
- **Headers & Wordmarks:** `Space Grotesk` or `Inter`. Weights: 600, 700.
- **Body & Data:** `JetBrains Mono` or `Fira Code`. Standardizing tabular and matrix outputs.

## Logo Usage
The Lemma logo geometrically combines the turnstile proof symbol (`⊢`) and the therefore symbol (`∴`) framed by structural scoping brackets.

- **`logo.svg`**: Transparent background primary logo. Usage: navigation bars, documents, diagrams.
- **`logo-dark-bg.svg`**: Void Black background logo. Usage: avatars, favicons.
- **`wordmark.svg`**: Logo accompanied by the "lemma." text.

### Dos and Donts
- ✅ **DO** use the logo strictly on Void Black or Chalk White backgrounds.
- ❌ **DON'T** round the corners of the structural brackets.
- ❌ **DON'T** recolor the `∴` (therefore) dots anything but Terminal Green or monochrome matching the turnstile.
- ❌ **DON'T** rasterize the logo. Only scale the mathematical SVG vectors.
